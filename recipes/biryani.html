Biryani

Description:

Biryani is a mixed rice dish originating in South Asia, made with rice, meat (chicken, goat, lamb, beef) or seafood (prawns or fish), and spices.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Meat
  • Yogurt
  • Onions
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Spices
  • Herbs

Steps:

  1. Marinate the Meat
  2. Cook the Rice
  3. Fry the Onions
  4. Cook the Meat
  5. Layer the Biryani
  6. Dum Cooking
  7. Garnish and Serve
