Fried-Rice

Description:

Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan and is usually mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Vegetables
  • Aromatics
  • Eggs
  • Sauces
  • Seasonings

Steps:

  1. Prepare the rice
  2. Stir-fry vegetables and aromatics
  3. Add other vegetables
  4. Scramble the eggs
  5. Add the rice
  6. Season and combine
  7. Garnish and serve
